Welcome to Celestial, Celestial is a webpage based around the exploration of human agency, with technology evolving faster than ever and the introduction of AI and generative art, it brings into question how much control we truly have in the results made by technology. Are prompts we give enough to dictate that the art we make by AI is truly ours.

I decided to use the metaphor of a celestial being as a way to represent that need for dominion as I feel that technology is becoming a power that is almost outside of our grasp. By using the cosmos, it provides a vast world that we can explore drawing many parallels to the near endless opportunities that technology can bring.

As a celestial being on this page, it is your job to navigate this world and obtain knowledge through the works showcased, strengthen your understanding of technology, gain a new appreciation for it, however the way you utilize this new-found power will be completely under your agency.
